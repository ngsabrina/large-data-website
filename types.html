Types of Jellyfish

There are some basic types of jellyfish that other jellyfish are categorized under. Some basic examples are: Hydrozoa, Stauromedusae, and Box Jellyfish.

Hydrozoa

Jellyfish can make a tasty meal for those of us willing to try something so exotic.

Stauromedusae

Most jellyfish are harmless, but there are some that can really hurt you.

Box Jellyfish

These jellyfish, also know as Aurelia aurita, are aptly named after their moon like appearance in the dark waters.
